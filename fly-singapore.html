Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 : Arrive In Singapore

Singapore is also known as "Lion City". Soon after arrival you will be taken into hotel to get fresh. Later on, same evening, you will be taken to the "Gardens by the Bay Tour".

Garden is perfect for a family or for a couple's, which has many attractions like cloud forest which is a mountain covered with diverse vegetation cloaking the world's tallest indoor waterfall, flower dome, super tree groove and many more.

Day 2 : Singapore City Tour

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for a half day guided tour of the colonial city which provides a short glance of city, history and people. Explore the city by driving through the Civic District passing the Padang, Cricket Club, Historic Parliament House, Supreme Court and City Hall. Continue drive to Merlion Park and enjoy a spectacular view of Marina Bay. Next stop at a local Handicraft center, then proceed to the National Orchid Garden, which showcases 60,000 varieties of Orchid plants.

The tour ends with a stop at Little India. Wander along the bustling streets with the scent of Joss sticks, Jasmine and spices. Later you can choose (on extra cost) to enjoy a river cruise, the highlights of the 40 min boat ride include Boat Quay, Clarke Quay and Marina Bay. You will be able to get a magnificent view of Singapore’s skyline.

You will come across and get close views of popular landmarks like Raffles Landing Site, The Merlion, The Esplanade and Marina Bay Sands during the tour. Evening is free for you to explore the beauty of this bustling cosmopolitan city on your own or take an optional night safari tour

Day 3 : Universal Studio and S.E.A Aquarium

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for a full day tour of Universal Studios Singapore with S.E.A Aquarium. It features movie-themed rides and attractions, which are unique. Thrill seekers and families will be the first to experience many new rides and shows based on blockbuster hits.

The young people and the people who are young at heart will be entertained by Shrek and gang on an incredible 4-D adventure in the world’s first Far Away Castle. Thrill seekers can face warrior mummies on Revenge of the Mummy, a psychological thrill ride in total darkness. Other highlights include Madagascar, a Crate Adventure and Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure

Day 4 : Southern Island

After breakfast at the hotel, morning is at leisure for you to relax or you can take an optional tour of Jurong Bird Park. This open concept park is the largest in the Asia Pacific region, with a collection of over 5,000 birds across 380 species. The highlight of the park is its unique penguin feeding session, which visitors can view through a large underwater viewing tank. Other than Penguin feeding, Jurong Bird Park also has many shows and entertainments to offer to its visitors.

In the afternoon proceed for a tour of Sentosa Island, an island that offers fun-filled activities for the entire family. This includes the Images of Singapore, a journey to the history of this vibrant city showcasing the transformation of a fishing village into a modern city.

The next stop that you can take is at the Dolphin Lagoon, to get a chance to meet the Pink dolphin and get astonished by its performance. Visit the Underwater World to feel the sharks and rays swimming right over your head in the magnificent glass aquarium and finally get enchanted by the "Songs of the Sea" a musical extravaganza comprising a stunning display of laser and lights

Day 5 : Dept from Singapore

After breakfast check out from the hotel and the day is free for you to do the last minute shopping. In the afternoon you will be transferred to the airport for your flight back home
